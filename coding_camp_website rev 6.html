About CodeCraft
A coding camp built by students, for students.

CodeCraft was born from a simple idea: the best people to teach kids to code are those who learned it recently themselves.

We designed every lesson from the perspective of a student — what's confusing, what clicks, and what actually makes coding fun. Our curriculum is project-first, meaning you build something real from day one.

Our mission is to make quality coding education accessible, affordable, and genuinely enjoyable for every middle schooler.
